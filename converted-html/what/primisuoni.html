Primisuoni

2011, Venice. A circuit bended electronic toy patched as a midi controller.

Electronic toy, Max/MSP.

Collaborators: Stefano Trento, powered by Maisongb

Primisuoni is a modified electronic toy that generates noise sand glitches. The video shows the attempt of interfacing it to Max/MSP in order to be used as midi controller.
